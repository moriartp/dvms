Design Matters

The 2015 Federal Employee Viewpoint Survey collected 0ver 400,000 responses from federal employees to learn about the culture and work environments of numerous agencies.

By aggregating information provided by individual employees we can understand the organizational strengths and weaknesses throughout the government.

With this information, strategic decisions may be made that will address organizational shortcomings as identified by the very people who make up our administrative offices
